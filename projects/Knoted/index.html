What exactly does Knoted do?

Knoted is a creative thinking tool that helps you generate ideas by collecting, organizing, and connecting visual, textual, and video inputs. You start with a simple prompt, and the AI suggests related stimuli. But unlike traditional AI tools, Knoted doesn’t just output content—it helps you explore your own thinking by creating meaningful connections between different elements through a system of interactive “nodes”.

Is Knoted just another AI image/text generator?

Not at all. Knoted isn’t focused on producing final content for you. Instead, it acts as a creative assistant that expands your thought process. The AI supports idea generation, surfaces hidden links between materials, and helps you structure your creative direction visually. It’s a tool to help you think , not just to automate.

Can I use my own files and references?

Yes—this is one of Knoted’s core features. You can upload images, PDFs, screenshots, notes, and more . The AI will analyze them and integrate them into the creative process, suggesting connections and related content. It’s not about starting from zero—it’s about building from what you already have.

Is Knoted made for individual creatives or teams?

Knoted works beautifully for individuals , especially designers, strategists, art directors, and students who want to explore and visualize ideas quickly. While team collaboration isn’t a core feature in this version, shared projects and team tools are planned for future development.

Will Knoted replace my design tools like Figma or Milanote?

No. Knoted is not a replacement, it’s a pre-step . It fits into the early stages of your creative process— ideation, research, inspiration, exploration —before you move into production tools like Figma, Adobe, or Milanote. Think of Knoted as a space where ideas take shape, so you can start designing with more clarity and direction.
